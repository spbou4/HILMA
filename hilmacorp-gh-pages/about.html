About Us

Hilma Corp was founded in 2023 by Massamba Maybe and Simon-Pierre Boucher. As a research and consulting firm specializing in finance, economics, and machine learning, Hilma Corp has quickly become a trusted partner for businesses, governments, and individuals seeking innovative solutions to complex challenges. The founders of Hilma Corp, Massamba and Simon-Pierre, bring a wealth of experience and expertise to the organization. As candidates for a PhD in finance, both have deep knowledge in finance and economics, as well as a passion for technology and innovation. Their vision and leadership have helped to establish Hilma Corp as a leading force in the research and consulting industry. At Hilma Corp, the team is committed to providing exceptional value to clients by delivering customized solutions tailored to their unique needs. With a focus on innovation and excellence, the team leverages cutting-edge technology and unparalleled expertise to help clients optimize financial performance, navigate complex economic challenges, and harness the power of machine learning to drive business outcomes. Hilma Corp's services include financial consulting, economic consulting, and machine learning consulting. These services are designed to help clients achieve their goals and stay ahead of the curve in today's fast-paced and ever-changing global marketplace. Overall, Hilma Corp is an organization committed to delivering exceptional value to its clients by providing customized solutions, leveraging technology and innovation, and harnessing the expertise of its team of professionals. The organization is well positioned to continue growing and evolving in the years to come.
